About Me

Hi, I’m Susie. I’m currently studying Graphic Design at Algonquin College. I just finished first year and loved it.

I am working part-time in a non-art or design-related field and moving towards changing my career. I have always loved art/design but never pursued it seriously until now.

My hobbies include cooking, tribal fusion belly dance, yoga, and photography. I also love to travel and have been to Paris, Nice, London, Amsterdam, Vancouver, the East Coast and parts of China.
